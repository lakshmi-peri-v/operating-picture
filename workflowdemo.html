This is a thinking tool, not a product. Pick any workflow — or name your own — and the same method resolves it: scattered signals become typed facts, specialized agents interpret them, a human approves, and every step stays on the record.

The figures are illustrative, but the operating model is real. At Intuit Fintech, I designed and deployed a multi-agent program operations capability that transformed fragmented execution signals into a governed operating picture for leaders — a shared source of truth for risks, dependencies, and execution health.

What you see here is the read. Behind it sits the full operating system — the same method run end-to-end across a live program, with the signal engine, the economics, and how it scales to many programs at once.
